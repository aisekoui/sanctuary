In the stillness of night, shadows softly unfold,
A quiet refuge where stories are told.
The glow of the screen, a familiar light,
Wrapping me gently in memory's sight.

The couch cradles the weight of thought,
In silence, comfort is effortlessly sought.
Echoes of voices, both distant and near,
Whisper solace only I can hear.

Here, time softens and slips away,
Beneath cinematic hues that sway.
My sanctuary in the dim-lit glow,
Where mind and heart quietly flow.
